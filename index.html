    Privacy Policy — Seekers

    Last Updated: July 5, 2026

    Your anonymity is our promise. You control what you share, when you share it, and with whom. We will never sell your data. We will never judge your story.

    1. Information We Collect

    When you use BetterBond as a seeker, we collect:

    • Account Information: Email address, phone number, and a username of your choice. Real names are optional until you choose to share them with a mentor.
    • Session Data: Problem categories you select, session notes (if you choose to save them), ratings, and feedback.
    • Communication Data: Chat transcripts, voice/video session metadata (duration, format), and any files you upload.
    • Payment Information: Processed securely through Razorpay / PayU. We do not store card details.
    • Technical Data: IP address, device type, browser information, and cookies for platform functionality.

    2. How We Use Your Information

    We use your data strictly for:

    • Matching you with the most suitable mentor couple based on problem type, language, and cultural background.
    • Processing payments and issuing refunds where applicable.
    • Improving our matching algorithm and platform experience.
    • Crisis escalation — if you mention self-harm, abuse, or severe mental health crisis, our trained escalation team will intervene and connect you to licensed professionals or emergency services.

    3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

    We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share data in these limited circumstances:

    • With Your Mentor: Only the problem category and language preference you selected. Your real name, email, and phone remain hidden until you explicitly choose to reveal them.
    • Service Providers: Payment gateways (Razorpay), cloud hosting (AWS India), and video infrastructure (Agora / Twilio) under strict confidentiality agreements.
    • Legal Compliance: If required by court order, law enforcement, or to prevent imminent harm to you or others.

    4. Your Rights

    As a seeker, you have the right to:

    • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
    • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information.
    • Deletion: Request account deletion. We will erase your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
    • Portability: Export your session history and notes.
    • Anonymity: Use a pseudonym throughout the platform. You may reveal your identity only when you feel ready.

    5. Data Security

    We implement industry-standard security measures:

    • End-to-end encryption for all chat and video sessions.
    • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit.
    • Data stored on AWS Mumbai region servers, compliant with Indian data localization requirements.
    • Regular security audits and penetration testing.
    • Strict access controls — only authorized personnel can view seeker data, and only for operational necessity.

    6. Session Recordings

    Voice and video sessions are never recorded by default. If you and your mentor both opt in, recordings are stored encrypted for 30 days for quality assurance, then permanently deleted. You may request deletion at any time.

    7. Cookies and Tracking

    We use essential cookies for login sessions and platform functionality. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising. You may disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

    8. Children's Privacy

    BetterBond is strictly for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover a minor has registered, we will delete the account and data immediately.

    9. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this policy as our platform evolves. We will notify you via email and in-app notification at least 15 days before any material changes take effect.

    10. Contact Us

    For privacy-related questions, data access requests, or complaints:

    • Email: privacy@betterbond.in
    • Postal: BetterBond Technologies Pvt. Ltd., [Address], India
    • Data Protection Officer: [Name], reachable at dpo@betterbond.in

    By using BetterBond, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

    Privacy Policy — Mentor Couples

    Last Updated: July 5, 2026

    Your safety is our foundation. We verify every mentor. We protect your identity. We ensure your data is used only for platform operations and never shared without consent.

    1. Information We Collect

    As a mentor couple, we collect more detailed information to ensure platform safety and quality:

    • Personal Information: Full names, ages, email addresses, phone numbers, city of residence, and occupations for both partners.
    • Verification Documents: Marriage certificate, identity proof (Aadhaar / Passport / Voter ID), address proof, and PAN card. These are stored encrypted and accessed only by authorized verification staff.
    • Relationship History: Details of challenges you have overcome, mentoring motivations, and topic preferences. This helps us match you with appropriate seekers.
    • Session Data: Number of sessions conducted, ratings received, earnings, and feedback from seekers.
    • Payment Information: Bank account details, PAN card, and UPI IDs for mentor payouts. Processed through secure payment infrastructure.
    • Technical Data: Login history, IP addresses, and device information for security monitoring.

    2. How We Use Your Information

    We use mentor data for:

    • Background verification and identity confirmation.
    • Matching you with seekers based on your stated expertise, language, and experience.
    • Processing mentor payouts and tax compliance (TDS as applicable under Indian law).
    • Quality assurance — monitoring ratings, session completion rates, and seeker feedback.
    • Community building — mentor-only forums, leaderboards, and recognition programs.
    • Legal protection — maintaining records in case of disputes or platform liability issues.

    3. Document Retention and Security

    Your verification documents are treated with the highest security:

    • Documents are stored using AES-256 encryption on AWS India servers.
    • Access is restricted to two authorized verification officers only.
    • Documents are reviewed once and then archived. They are not accessed again unless legally required.
    • If your application is rejected, all documents are permanently deleted within 30 days.
    • If you leave the platform, documents are retained for 1 year for legal compliance, then deleted.

    4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

    We share mentor data only in these limited circumstances:

    • With Seekers: Only your first names, a short bio you write, your mentor tier, and your topic specialties. Your full name, address, phone, and documents are never shared.
    • Payment Processors: Bank details shared with Razorpay / PayU for payouts only.
    • Legal Compliance: If required by court order, tax authorities, or law enforcement under valid legal process.
    • Insurance: Anonymized session data may be shared with our liability insurance provider for risk assessment.

    5. Your Rights

    As a mentor couple, you have the right to:

    • Access: Request a complete copy of all data we hold about you and your partner.
    • Correction: Update your bio, availability, topic preferences, and contact details at any time.
    • Withdrawal: Leave the platform with 15 days notice. Pending payouts will be settled within 30 days.
    • Document Deletion: Request deletion of verification documents after your tenure ends (subject to legal retention periods).
    • Dispute: Challenge any negative rating or feedback through our mentor appeal process.

    6. Mentor Code of Conduct and Data

    By joining, you agree:

    • You will not share seeker identities, stories, or session details outside the platform.
    • You will not solicit seekers for personal relationships, business, or off-platform services.
    • You will not record sessions without explicit mutual consent.
    • You will escalate any mention of abuse, self-harm, or severe mental health crisis immediately to our professional escalation team.
    • Violation of these terms results in immediate removal and potential legal action.

    7. Payment and Tax Compliance

    We comply with all Indian tax regulations:

    • TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) at applicable rates will be deducted from mentor payouts as per Section 194J of the Income Tax Act.
    • Form 16A will be issued annually for tax filing purposes.
    • GST registration may be required for mentors earning above the threshold. We will guide you through this.
    • Payouts are processed monthly for verified mentors, bi-weekly for Expert mentors.

    8. Liability and Insurance

    BetterBond maintains professional liability insurance covering:

    • Platform errors or data breaches.
    • Disputes arising from mentor-seeker interactions (within platform guidelines).
    • We do not cover mentors who violate platform policies or provide advice outside their stated expertise.

    We may update this policy to reflect new legal requirements or platform features. Mentor couples will be notified via email and in-app notification at least 30 days before material changes.
